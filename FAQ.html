FAQ

Burger Daddies' Frequently Asked Question
WHO IS BURGER DADDIES?

First off, hello! Burger Daddies was started by 5 students from TARUMT in 2022. We're in the business of rolled, whole leaves, and giving you the best burgers in town.

GET IN TOUCH?

Want to get in touch? Brilliant stuff. Give us a shout on 016 521 6310. Or shoot us an email to hello@burgerdaddies.co.my and we'll be in touch. Speak soon.

CAN I VISIT BURGER DADDIES HQ?

Sadly not right now. Cause all of our top secret burger recipes are stored in our HQ. Yea~ Secret~

WHAT PAYMENT METHOD CAN I USE?

The lot. You can pay by credit or debit card {Visa, Mastercard, Maestro, Amex}, PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay, Shop Pay. We've got you covered. That being said, you can't pay for subscriptions via PayPal. Sorry about that. So make sure you've got your card to hand.

HOW DO I CHANGE SOMETHING ON AN ORDER I'VE JUST PLACED?

Just get in touch - call us at 016 521 6310 and we'll do our best to change what we can.

HOW MUCH DOES DELIVERY FEES COST?

A min charge of RM2, and a max charge of RM15. Delivery fees are charge upon the distance counted from the nearest Burger Daddies store around you.

WHEN WILL I GET MY ORDERS?

We don't like to keep you waiting, so we send all orders placed within 30 minutes. If it's been more than 30 minutes, let us know & we'll follow up you out. But sometimes due to some traffic issues and weather issues it's a little longer. We do appritiet your patient on waiting for your orders to arrive.

ANY JOB VACANCY AVAILBLE?

If you wish to join our Burger Daddies big family, do feel free to fill in the form of job enquiry in the 'Career Opportunities' page.

I HAVE QUESTION YOU HAVEN'T ANSWERED IN THIS LIST?

No worries, give us a shout on 016 637 9747 or email us at hello@burgerdaddies.co.my and we'll give you a hand.
